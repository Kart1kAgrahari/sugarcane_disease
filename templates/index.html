Bacterial Blight

This disease causes elongated brown streaks on the leaves, often surrounded by yellow halos. It can lead to reduced sugar content and affect crop yield.

Learn More

Mosaic

Mosaic disease is characterized by patchy light and dark green patterns on the leaves. It's caused by a virus and can lead to stunted growth and reduced yield.

Learn More

RedRot

RedRot causes internal discoloration and red streaks within the stalk, often with a foul odor. It can result in significant crop loss.

Learn More

Rust

Rust manifests as orange to reddish-brown pustules on the leaves, often leading to premature leaf drop and reduced photosynthesis.

Learn More

Yellow Leaf

Yellow Leaf disease turns leaves yellow, especially near the midrib. It's caused by a phytoplasma and can severely impact sugar production.
